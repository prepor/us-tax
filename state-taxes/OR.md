# Oregon (OR) - Sales Tax Guide for Online Retailers

## Overview
- **Has sales tax:** No. Oregon does not have a general sales or use tax.
- **State-level sales tax rate:** 0% (no sales tax)
- **Combined average rate (state + local):** 0%

Sources: [Oregon Department of Revenue - Sales Tax in Oregon](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/sales-tax.aspx)

## Economic Nexus Thresholds
- **Not applicable.** Because Oregon has no general sales tax, there are no economic nexus thresholds for sales tax collection.
- Online retailers shipping goods to Oregon customers do not need to collect any sales tax on those sales.

**Important note for Oregon-based sellers:** If you are an Oregon-based business selling to customers in other states that have sales tax, the Wayfair decision may still require you to collect and remit sales tax to those states if you meet their respective economic nexus thresholds.

Sources: [Oregon Department of Revenue - Sales Tax in Oregon](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/sales-tax.aspx)

## Tax Registration
- **Not applicable.** There is no sales tax permit or registration needed for sales tax purposes in Oregon.
- Oregon businesses may still need to register for other state taxes (e.g., Corporate Activity Tax, income tax).
- **Oregon Department of Revenue portal:** [Revenue Online](https://revenueonline.dor.oregon.gov/tap/_/)

Sources: [Oregon Department of Revenue - Businesses](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/default.aspx)

## Taxable Goods
- **Are physical goods generally taxable?** No. Oregon does not impose a general sales tax on tangible personal property.
- **Notable exemptions:** Not applicable since there is no sales tax.
- **Exception:** Oregon does have a vehicle use tax that applies to new vehicles purchased outside the state. The tax must be paid before the vehicle can be titled and registered in Oregon.

Sources: [Oregon Department of Revenue - Sales Tax in Oregon](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/sales-tax.aspx)

## Local Taxes
- **Do cities/counties have local sales tax?** No. No city or county in Oregon levies a general sales tax.
- **Note:** Some Oregon localities may impose other specific taxes (e.g., Multnomah County's Preschool for All tax, Metro's Supportive Housing Services tax), but these are income-based taxes, not sales taxes.

Sources: [Oregon Department of Revenue - Sales Tax in Oregon](https://www.oregon.gov/dor/programs/businesses/pages/sales-tax.aspx)

## Filing Requirements
- **Not applicable.** Since there is no sales tax, there are no sales tax filing requirements for sales made in Oregon.
