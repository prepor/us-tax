# Virginia (VA) - Sales Tax Guide for Online Retailers

## Overview
- **Has sales tax:** Yes
- **State-level sales tax rate:** 4.3% (state) + 1% (local, mandatory statewide) = 5.3% general rate
- **Regional rates:**
  - 5.3% general rate (most of the state)
  - 6% in Northern Virginia, Hampton Roads, and Central Virginia (Charles City County, Chesterfield County, Goochland County, Hanover County, Henrico County, New Kent County, Powhatan County, and the City of Richmond)
  - 6.3% in Charlotte County, City of Danville, Gloucester County, Halifax County, Henry County, Northampton County, Patrick County, and Pittsylvania County
  - 7% in the Historic Triangle (Williamsburg, James City County, York County)
- **Combined average rate (state + local):** Ranges from 5.3% to 7% depending on locality

Source: [Retail Sales and Use Tax | Virginia Tax](https://www.tax.virginia.gov/retail-sales-and-use-tax)

## Economic Nexus Thresholds
- **Revenue threshold:** $100,000 in annual gross revenue from retail sales in Virginia
- **Transaction threshold:** 200 or more separate transactions to Virginia customers per year
- **When the law took effect:** July 1, 2019
- **Legal basis:** Va. Code 58.1-612(D), enacted via House Bill 1722 (2019 Acts of Assembly, Chapter 815) and Senate Bill 1083 (2019 Acts of Assembly, Chapter 816)
- **Link to official nexus information:** [Remote Sellers, Marketplace Facilitators, Economic Nexus | Virginia Tax](https://www.tax.virginia.gov/remote-sellers-marketplace-facilitators-economic-nexus)

## Tax Registration
- **How to register:** Register online through Virginia Tax's iReg system or by mail using the Business Registration Application (Form R-1)
- **Registration portal:** [iReg - Virginia Tax Online Registration](https://www.ireg.tax.virginia.gov/)
- **Registration fees:** No fee noted for standard sales tax registration
- **What you receive:** A 15-digit sales tax account number and a Sales Tax Certificate of Registration (Form ST-4)
- **Note for remote sellers:** If you register as an out-of-state dealer, your account number will begin with 12

Source: [Register a Business in Virginia | Virginia Tax](https://www.tax.virginia.gov/register-business-virginia)

## Taxable Goods
- **Are physical goods generally taxable?** Yes
- **Notable exemptions:**
  - **Food for home consumption (groceries):** Taxed at a reduced rate of 1% (as of January 1, 2023). See [Grocery Tax | Virginia Tax](https://www.tax.virginia.gov/grocery-tax)
  - **Essential personal hygiene products:** Also taxed at the reduced 1% rate
  - **Prescription drugs:** Exempt
  - **Clothing:** Taxable at the full rate (no general clothing exemption)
  - **Prepared food/meals:** Taxable at the full rate (localities may also impose a meals tax)
- **Link to official exemptions:** [Retail Sales and Use Tax | Virginia Tax](https://www.tax.virginia.gov/retail-sales-and-use-tax)

## Local Taxes
- **Can cities/counties add local sales tax?** Virginia has a mandatory 1% local tax included in all rates statewide. In addition, certain regions have additional regional taxes:
  - Northern Virginia: +0.7% (total 6%)
  - Hampton Roads: +0.7% (total 6%)
  - Central Virginia (Charles City County, Chesterfield County, Goochland County, Hanover County, Henrico County, New Kent County, Powhatan County, and the City of Richmond): +0.7% (total 6%)
  - Charlotte County, City of Danville, Gloucester County, Halifax County, Henry County, Northampton County, Patrick County, and Pittsylvania County: +1.0% (total 6.3%)
  - Historic Triangle: +1.7% (total 7%)
- **Range of local/regional rates:** 1% to 2.7% (included in the total rates of 5.3% to 7% above)
- **Remote seller local tax collection:** Yes, remote sellers must collect tax at the rate applicable to the destination of the shipment
- **Rate lookup tool:** [Sales Tax Rate and Locality Code Lookup | Virginia Tax](https://www.tax.virginia.gov/sales-tax-rate-and-locality-code-lookup)

## Filing Requirements
- **Filing frequency:**
  - Monthly: If you owe $100 or more per month on average
  - Quarterly: If you owe less than $100 per month on average
  - Annual: If you owe less than $600 per year
- **Filing deadlines:** Returns are due on the 20th of the month following the close of the filing period
- **Filing portal:** [eForms | Virginia Tax](https://www.tax.virginia.gov/eforms) or [Virginia Tax Online Services for Businesses](https://www.business.tax.virginia.gov/VTOL/tax/Login.xhtml)
- **Web Upload option:** [Web Upload | Virginia Tax](https://www.tax.virginia.gov/web-upload)
