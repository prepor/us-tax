# Minnesota (MN) - Sales Tax Guide for Online Retailers

## Overview
- **Has sales tax:** Yes
- **State-level sales tax rate:** 6.875%
- **Combined average rate (state + local):** Varies by location; local rates range from 0.25% to 1.5% on top of the state rate, making the combined rate typically between 6.875% and 8.375%

Source: [Sales and Use Tax | Minnesota Department of Revenue](https://www.revenue.state.mn.us/sales-and-use-tax)

## Economic Nexus Thresholds
- **Revenue threshold:** $100,000 in retail sales shipped into Minnesota over a period of 12 consecutive months
- **Transaction threshold:** 200 or more retail sales shipped to Minnesota over a period of 12 consecutive months
- **When the law took effect:** October 1, 2019 (for marketplace providers; remote sellers required to collect starting with the small seller exception update)
- **Note:** Either threshold triggers the obligation to register and collect tax.
- **Link to official nexus information:** [Nexus (Taxable Presence) | Minnesota Department of Revenue](https://www.revenue.state.mn.us/nexus-minnesota-taxes)

## Tax Registration
- **How to register:** Apply online through the Minnesota Department of Revenue's Business Tax Registration system. Web registrations are instant; you will receive your Minnesota Tax ID Number within seconds of completing the registration. You may also register by phone.
- **Official registration portal:** [Registering Your Business | Minnesota Department of Revenue](https://www.revenue.state.mn.us/guide/registering-your-business)
- **Registration by phone:** Call Business Registration at 651-282-5225 or 1-800-657-3605 (toll-free)
- **Registration fees:** No fee mentioned in official resources.

Source: [Who Needs to Register? | Minnesota Department of Revenue](https://www.revenue.state.mn.us/guide/who-needs-register)

## Taxable Goods
- **Are physical goods generally taxable?** Yes. Sales of tangible personal property are generally subject to the 6.875% state sales tax (plus applicable local taxes).
- **Notable exemptions:**
  - **Clothing:** Exempt from sales tax, including sewing materials (fabric, thread, zippers, buttons, trim, etc.) used to make clothing.
  - **Food/Groceries:** Food and food ingredients are exempt. However, candy, soft drinks, dietary supplements, and prepared foods remain taxable.
  - **Prescription drugs:** Exempt.
  - **Over-the-counter analgesics:** Exempt.
- **Link to official exemption information:** [Nontaxable Sales | Minnesota Department of Revenue](https://www.revenue.state.mn.us/guide/nontaxable-sales)

Additional reference: [Minn. Stat. 297A.67](https://www.revisor.mn.gov/statutes/cite/297A.67)

## Local Taxes
- **Can cities/counties add local sales tax?** Yes. Some cities and counties in Minnesota impose local sales and use taxes.
- **Range of local rates:** Typically 0.25% to 1.5%
- **Do remote sellers need to collect local tax?** Yes. Local sales taxes apply to the same items and services as the general state sales tax. Remote sellers must collect applicable local taxes.
- **Rate lookup tool:** [Sales Tax Rate Calculator | Minnesota Department of Revenue](https://www.revenue.state.mn.us/sales-tax-rate-calculator)

Source: [Local Sales Tax Information | Minnesota Department of Revenue](https://www.revenue.state.mn.us/local-sales-tax-information)

Additional reference: [Local Sales and Use Tax Rate Guide (Q2 2026)](https://www.revenue.state.mn.us/sites/default/files/2026-02/local-sales-and-use-tax-rate-guide-2026-q2.pdf)

## Filing Requirements
- **Filing frequency:** Based on your monthly sales and use tax liability (including local taxes):
  - **Monthly:** Default filing schedule for most retailers
  - **Quarterly:** If averaging $500 or less per month in sales/use tax liability (must request authorization)
  - **Annual:** If averaging $100 or less per month in sales/use tax liability (must request authorization)
- **Filing deadlines:** Returns are due on or before the 20th day of the month following the close of the reporting period.
- **Filing portal:** [e-Services for Sales and Use Tax | Minnesota Department of Revenue](https://www.revenue.state.mn.us/e-services-sales-and-use-tax) (online filing is required)

Source: [Filing Information | Minnesota Department of Revenue](https://www.revenue.state.mn.us/guide/filing-information)
