# Wyoming (WY) - Sales Tax Guide for Online Retailers

## Overview
- **Has sales tax:** Yes
- **State-level sales tax rate:** 4%
- **Combined average rate (state + local):** Ranges from 4% to approximately 6% depending on county/city. For example, Cheyenne is 6%; Sheridan is 5.5%.

Source: [Tax Rates | Wyoming Excise Tax Division](https://excise-tax-div.wyo.gov/salesuselodging-tax/tax-rates)

## Economic Nexus Thresholds
- **Revenue threshold:** $100,000 in gross revenue from sales of tangible personal property, admissions, or services delivered into Wyoming in the current or immediately preceding calendar year
- **Transaction threshold:** None (the 200-transaction threshold was eliminated effective July 1, 2024)
- **What counts toward the threshold:** All sales delivered to Wyoming customers, whether taxable or exempt, including wholesale and resale transactions
- **When the law originally took effect:** July 1, 2019 (originally with both revenue and transaction thresholds)
- **Link to official information:** [Excise Tax FAQs | Wyoming Excise Tax Division](https://excise-tax-div.wyo.gov/general-administrative/excise-tax-faqs)

Note: The elimination of the 200-transaction threshold was confirmed by multiple official sources. See [Wyoming Excise Tax Division](https://excise-tax-div.wyo.gov/) for the most current rules.

## Tax Registration
- **How to register:** Register online through the Wyoming Internet Filing System (WYIFS)
- **Registration portal:** [WYIFS | Wyoming Excise Tax Division](https://excise-tax-div.wyo.gov/wyifs)
- **New vendor information:** [New Vendor Packets | Wyoming Excise Tax Division](https://excise-tax-div.wyo.gov/new-vendor-packets)
- **Registration fees:** No specific registration fee noted on official sources; confirm with Vendor Operations at (307) 777-5200
- **Streamlined Sales Tax:** Wyoming is a member of the Streamlined Sales Tax Project, and remote sellers may also register through the [SSTRS](https://www.streamlinedsalestax.org/). See [Streamlined Sales Tax Project | Wyoming Excise Tax Division](https://excise-tax-div.wyo.gov/streamlined-sales-tax-project)

## Taxable Goods
- **Are physical goods generally taxable?** Yes
- **Notable exemptions:**
  - **Groceries / food for human consumption:** Exempt from sales tax
  - **Prescription drugs:** Exempt
  - **Over-the-counter drugs:** Taxable at the 4% state rate
  - **Clothing:** Taxable at the full rate (no general clothing exemption)
  - **Food purchased with SNAP benefits:** Exempt
- **Link to official information:** [Excise Tax FAQs | Wyoming Excise Tax Division](https://excise-tax-div.wyo.gov/general-administrative/excise-tax-faqs) and [Sales/Use/Lodging Tax | Wyoming Excise Tax Division](https://excise-tax-div.wyo.gov/salesuselodging-tax)

Note: For a comprehensive list of exemptions, refer to Wyoming Statute 39-15-105 (sales tax exemptions). A summary is available from the [Wyoming Legislature (PDF)](https://wyoleg.gov/InterimCommittee/2024/03-20240528WYDOR-Sales-UseTaxExemptions5.29.24.pdf).

## Local Taxes
- **Can counties/cities add local sales tax?** Yes, counties can impose additional local taxes
- **Range of local rates:** 0% to 2%, making the combined rate range 4% to 6%
- **Examples:** Cheyenne (Laramie County) 6%; Sheridan 5.5%; Cody 4%
- **Remote seller local tax collection:** Yes, remote sellers must collect sales tax at the rate applicable to the delivery destination
- **Rate lookup tool:** [Wyoming Rate Lookup | WY DOR](https://ratelookup.wyo.gov/)
- **Rate charts:** [Sales & Use Tax Rate Charts | Wyoming Excise Tax Division](https://excise-tax-div.wyo.gov/sales-use-tax-rate-charts)

## Filing Requirements
- **Filing frequency:** Assigned by the Wyoming Department of Revenue based on sales volume:
  - Monthly: Average monthly liability greater than $150
  - Quarterly: Average monthly liability between $50 and $150
  - Annual: Average monthly liability less than $50
- **Filing deadlines:** Returns are due the last day of the month following the reporting period
- **Filing portal:** [WYIFS | Wyoming Excise Tax Division](https://excise-tax-div.wyo.gov/wyifs) (supports ACH Debit, E-Check, and credit card payments)
- **E-services:** [Excise Tax E-Services | Wyoming Excise Tax Division](https://excise-tax-div.wyo.gov/excise-tax-e-services)
