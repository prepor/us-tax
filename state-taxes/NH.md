# New Hampshire (NH) - Sales Tax Guide for Online Retailers

## Overview
- **Does New Hampshire have a sales tax?** No
- **State-level sales tax rate:** 0% (no general sales tax)
- **Combined average rate (state + local):** 0% (no general sales tax)

New Hampshire is one of five U.S. states with no general sales tax. Online retailers selling physical goods into New Hampshire are **not required** to collect sales tax.

Source: [Does New Hampshire have a sales tax? - NH Department of Revenue Administration](https://www.revenue.nh.gov/faq/does-new-hampshire-have-sales-tax)

## Economic Nexus Thresholds
- **Not applicable.** New Hampshire does not impose a general sales or use tax, so there are no economic nexus thresholds for sales tax collection.

New Hampshire has actively opposed the collection of sales tax by other states from New Hampshire-based businesses. The state has taken legal and legislative action to protect its businesses from other states' sales tax collection requirements.

Source: [New Hampshire to Fight Internet Sales Tax - Governor's Office](https://www.governor.nh.gov/news-and-media/new-hampshire-fight-internet-sales-tax)

## Tax Registration
- **Not applicable.** Since New Hampshire has no general sales tax, remote sellers do not need to register for a sales tax permit in New Hampshire.
- New Hampshire does have other business taxes (Business Profits Tax, Business Enterprise Tax), but these apply to businesses with nexus in New Hampshire, not to the collection of sales tax on shipped goods.

Source: [Business Taxes - NH DRA](https://www.revenue.nh.gov/taxes-glance/business-taxes)

## Taxable Goods
- **Are physical goods generally taxable?** No. New Hampshire does not impose a general sales tax on tangible personal property.
- There are no general sales tax exemptions to list because no general sales tax exists.

**Note:** New Hampshire does have some limited taxes that do not apply to online retail sales of physical goods:
- **Meals and Rentals Tax (8.5%):** Applies to restaurant meals, hotel/motel rooms, and motor vehicle rentals within New Hampshire. This does not apply to online retail sales of physical goods.

Source: [Taxes at a Glance - NH DRA](https://www.revenue.nh.gov/taxes-glance)

## Local Taxes
- **Can cities/counties add local sales tax?** No. There is no general sales tax at any level in New Hampshire (state, county, or municipal).
- **Range of local rates:** N/A
- **Do remote sellers need to collect local tax?** No.

Source: [Taxes at a Glance - NH DRA](https://www.revenue.nh.gov/taxes-glance)

## Filing Requirements
- **Not applicable** for sales tax. There are no sales tax returns to file for online retail sales into New Hampshire.
