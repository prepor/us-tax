# District of Columbia (DC) - Sales Tax Guide for Online Retailers

## Overview
- **Has sales tax:** Yes
- **Sales tax rate:** 6.0% on tangible personal property, digital goods, and taxable services (through September 30, 2026). **Increasing to 7.0% effective October 1, 2026.**
- **Special rates:** 8% on soft drinks; 10% on restaurant meals, liquor for on-premises consumption, and rental vehicles; 10.25% on alcoholic beverages for off-premises consumption
- **Combined average rate:** DC has no local taxes beyond the district-wide rate, so the rate is uniform at 6.0% (7.0% after October 1, 2026)

Source: [Sales & Use Tax | DC OTR](https://otr.cfo.dc.gov/page/sales-use-tax), [District of Columbia Tax Changes Take Effect October 1st | DC OTR](https://otr.cfo.dc.gov/release/district-columbia-tax-changes-take-effect-october1-24), and [Notice of October 1, 2025 Tax Changes | DC OTR](https://otr.cfo.dc.gov/release/notice-oct-1-2025-tax-changes) (confirms that the 6.0% general rate continues through September 30, 2026, and that the previously announced intermediate 6.5% step was cancelled)

## Economic Nexus Thresholds
- **Revenue threshold:** More than $100,000 in gross receipts from retail sales delivered into the District per year
- **Transaction threshold:** 200 or more separate retail sales delivered into the District per year
- **Measurement period:** Previous calendar year or current calendar year (either triggers the obligation)
- **When the law took effect:** January 1, 2019
- **Link to official nexus information:** [Marketplace Sellers FAQs | DC OTR](https://otr.cfo.dc.gov/page/marketplace-sellers-frequently-asked-questions-faqs)
- **Nexus guidance:** [OTR's Guidance for Questions Involving Nexus (PDF)](https://otr.cfo.dc.gov/sites/default/files/dc/sites/otr/publication/attachments/tax_guide_nexus.pdf)

## Tax Registration
- **How to register:** File a Combined Business Tax Registration Application (Form FR-500) with the Office of Tax and Revenue
- **Registration portal:** [MyTax DC](https://mytax.dc.gov/_/)
- **Registration fees:** No charge for registering
- **Additional information:** [New Business Registration | DC OTR](https://otr.cfo.dc.gov/page/new-business-registration)

## Taxable Goods
- **Are physical goods generally taxable?** Yes
- **Notable exemptions:**
  - **Groceries / unprepared food:** Exempt (fresh produce, bread, pantry staples, and other items qualifying for SNAP benefits)
  - **Prepared food / restaurant meals:** Taxable at 10%
  - **Prescription drugs:** Exempt
  - **Non-prescription drugs:** Exempt
  - **Clothing:** Taxable at the standard rate (6.0%; 7.0% after October 1, 2026)
  - **Vitamins and nutritional supplements (not prepared for immediate consumption):** Not subject to sales tax
- **Official food guidance:** [OTR's Guidance for Sales Tax Concerning Food (PDF)](https://otr.cfo.dc.gov/sites/default/files/dc/sites/otr/publication/attachments/tax_guide_food.pdf)
- **Taxable and non-taxable services:** [Taxable and Non-Taxable Services | DC OTR](https://otr.cfo.dc.gov/page/taxable-and-non-taxable-services)
- **Exemptions:** [Exemptions - Audit Division | DC OTR](https://otr.cfo.dc.gov/page/exemptions-audit-division)

## Local Taxes
- **Can localities add local sales tax?** No -- DC is a single jurisdiction with no separate city/county taxes. The sales tax rate is uniform district-wide.
- **Range of local rates:** N/A (no additional local taxes)
- **Remote seller local tax collection:** Not applicable; only one rate applies across the entire District

## Filing Requirements
- **Filing frequency:** Based on your sales and use tax liability:
  - **Monthly** (Form FR-800M): If tax liability is $1,201 or more per period
  - **Quarterly** (Form FR-800Q): If tax liability is $201 to $1,200 per period
  - **Annual** (Form FR-800A): If tax liability is $200 or less per period
- **Filing deadlines:** Returns and payments are due by the 20th day of the month following the end of the reporting period
- **Filing portal:** [MyTax DC](https://mytax.dc.gov/_/)
- **Sales and use tax returns:** [Sales and Use Tax Returns | DC OTR](https://otr.cfo.dc.gov/page/sales-and-use-tax-returns)
- **Sales and use tax instructions:** [Sales and Use Tax Instructions | DC OTR](https://otr.cfo.dc.gov/page/sales-and-use-tax-instructions)
