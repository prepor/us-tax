# Ohio (OH) - Sales Tax Guide for Online Retailers

## Overview
- **Has sales tax:** Yes
- **State-level sales tax rate:** 5.75% ([The Finder - Sales Tax](https://thefinder.tax.ohio.gov/StreamlineSalesTaxWeb/))
- **Combined average rate (state + local):** Approximately 7.25%. Combined rates range from 6.50% to 8.00% depending on county ([County Rate Table - OH Tax Dept](https://thefinder.tax.ohio.gov/streamlinesalestaxweb/Download/BoundaryData/CountySalesTaxRateReport.pdf))

## Economic Nexus Thresholds
- **Revenue threshold:** $100,000 in gross receipts from sales into Ohio in the current or preceding calendar year ([Sales and Use Tax - Nexus - Out of State Sellers](https://tax.ohio.gov/wps/portal/gov/tax/professional/OHTaxAlert/TaxAlertsArchive/SalesUseNexus))
- **Transaction threshold:** 200 or more separate transactions in the current or preceding calendar year ([Sales Tax Institute - Ohio Economic Nexus](https://www.salestaxinstitute.com/resources/ohio-enacts-new-economic-nexus-provisions-and-marketplace-nexus-legislation))
- **Either threshold triggers nexus:** Yes, meeting either the $100,000 revenue OR 200 transaction threshold is sufficient
- **Effective date:** August 1, 2019 (under Amended Sub. H.B. 166) ([Sales Tax Institute - Ohio Economic Nexus](https://www.salestaxinstitute.com/resources/ohio-enacts-new-economic-nexus-provisions-and-marketplace-nexus-legislation))
- **Registration timing:** Registration is required on the first day of the month following at least 30 days after crossing either threshold ([Avalara - Ohio Economic Nexus](https://www.avalara.com/blog/en/north-america/2021/12/which-sales-count-toward-ohios-economic-nexus-threshold.html))
- **Official nexus guidance:** [Sales and Use Tax - Nexus - Out of State Sellers](https://tax.ohio.gov/wps/portal/gov/tax/professional/OHTaxAlert/TaxAlertsArchive/SalesUseNexus)

## Tax Registration
- **How to register:** Two options:
  1. **Ohio Business Gateway:** Register for a vendor's license through the Ohio Business Gateway ([Ohio Business Gateway](https://ohiobusinessgateway.ohio.gov/))
  2. **Streamlined Sales Tax Registration:** As a full SST member state, Ohio accepts registration through [www.sstregister.org](https://www.sstregister.org) ([Use Tax, Streamlined Sales Tax - OH Tax Dept](https://tax.ohio.gov/business/sales-and-use-tax/use-tax-streamlined-sales-tax-sales-vda-sales-tax-holiday))
- **Registration portal:** [Ohio Business Gateway](https://ohiobusinessgateway.ohio.gov/) or [OH|TAX eServices](https://myportal.tax.ohio.gov/TAP/_/)
- **Registration fees:** No fee for remote sellers registering via Streamlined Sales Tax or for consumer's use tax accounts ([Use Tax, Streamlined Sales Tax - OH Tax Dept](https://tax.ohio.gov/business/sales-and-use-tax/use-tax-streamlined-sales-tax-sales-vda-sales-tax-holiday))

## Taxable Goods
- **Are physical goods generally taxable?** Yes. Retail sales of tangible personal property are generally subject to sales tax ([The Finder - Sales Tax](https://thefinder.tax.ohio.gov/StreamlineSalesTaxWeb/))
- **Notable exemptions:**
  - **Food/groceries:** Food for human consumption off the premises where sold is exempt. Exceptions: alcoholic beverages, dietary supplements, soft drinks, tobacco, and prepared food ([OH Tax Dept - Sales Tax FAQ](https://www.tax.ohio.gov/sales_and_use/faqs/sales_basics/tabid/3095/Default.aspx?AFMID=9887&QuestionID=433))
  - **Clothing:** Generally taxable at the full rate. Ohio has an annual sales tax holiday (typically in August) during which most tangible personal property priced at $500 or less is exempt -- not just clothing. Clothing specifically has no price cap during the holiday. The expanded holiday (2024-2025) covers most goods including electronics, furniture, and restaurant menu items under $500. ([Ohio Sales Tax Holiday](https://tax.ohio.gov/business/sales-and-use-tax/use-tax-streamlined-sales-tax-sales-vda-sales-tax-holiday))
  - **Prescription drugs:** Drugs dispensed pursuant to a prescription for human use are exempt ([OH Tax Dept - Sales Tax FAQ](https://www.tax.ohio.gov/sales_and_use/faqs/sales_basics/tabid/3095/Default.aspx?AFMID=9887&QuestionID=433))
- **Official reference:** [OH Tax Dept - FAQs](https://tax.ohio.gov/help-center/faqs)

## Local Taxes
- **Can counties add local sales tax?** Yes. Ohio counties may levy permissive sales and use taxes ([County Rate Table - OH Tax Dept](https://thefinder.tax.ohio.gov/streamlinesalestaxweb/Download/BoundaryData/CountySalesTaxRateReport.pdf))
- **Range of local (county) rates:** 0.75% to 2.25%, resulting in combined rates of 6.50% to 8.00% ([Total State and Local Sales Tax Rates by County (PDF)](https://dam.assets.ohio.gov/image/upload/tax.ohio.gov/tax_analysis/tax_data_series/sales_and_use/Salestaxmap.pdf))
- **Remote seller local tax collection:** Yes. Ohio is a destination-based state; remote sellers must collect the combined state + county rate based on the delivery address. Use "The Finder" tool to look up rates by address or ZIP code ([The Finder - Lookup By Address](https://thefinder.tax.ohio.gov/StreamlineSalesTaxWeb/AddressLookup/LookupByAddress.aspx?taxType=Sales))

## Filing Requirements
- **Filing frequency:** Monthly or semi-annually, based on annual tax liability. The Department assigns your filing frequency ([Online Services - Business Taxes](https://tax.ohio.gov/business/sales-and-use-tax/use-tax-streamlined-sales-tax-sales-vda-sales-tax-holiday))
- **Filing deadlines:** Returns are due on the **23rd day of the month** following the end of the reporting period ([Online Services - Business Taxes](https://tax.ohio.gov/business/sales-and-use-tax/use-tax-streamlined-sales-tax-sales-vda-sales-tax-holiday))
- **Filing portal:** [Ohio Business Gateway](https://ohiobusinessgateway.ohio.gov/) or [OH|TAX eServices](https://myportal.tax.ohio.gov/TAP/_/)
