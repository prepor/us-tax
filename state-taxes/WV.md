# West Virginia (WV) - Sales Tax Guide for Online Retailers

## Overview
- **Has sales tax:** Yes
- **State-level sales tax rate:** 6%
- **Combined average rate (state + local):** 6% to 7% (state 6% plus up to 1% municipal tax where applicable)

Source: [Sales and Use Tax | WV Tax Division](https://tax.wv.gov/business/salesandusetax/pages/salesandusetax.aspx)

## Economic Nexus Thresholds
- **Revenue threshold:** More than $100,000 in annual gross sales delivered in West Virginia
- **Transaction threshold:** 200 or more separate sales transactions delivered in West Virginia per year
- **Small-seller exception:** A remote seller with $100,000 or less in annual gross sales AND fewer than 200 separate transactions is not required to register and collect tax
- **When the law took effect:** January 1, 2019
- **Link to official nexus information:** [Remote Sellers and West Virginia Sales and Use Tax](https://tax.wv.gov/business/salesandusetax/ecommerce/remotesellers/Pages/RemoteSellersAndWestVirginiaTax.aspx)

## Tax Registration
- **How to register:** Two options:
  1. Register directly with West Virginia online at [MyTaxes](https://mytaxes.wvtax.gov)
  2. Register through the [Streamlined Sales Tax Registration System (SSTRS)](https://www.streamlinedsalestax.org/) to register for multiple states
- **One-stop registration:** Register with all necessary WV agencies at [Business4WV](https://business4.wv.gov)
- **Registration fees:** $30 registration tax (exceptions for charitable organizations, government agencies, agricultural/farming activities, and "withholding only" accounts)
- **Official registration information:** [Business Registration | WV Tax Division](https://tax.wv.gov/business/businessregistration/pages/businessregistration.aspx)

Source: [TSD 317 - Registration and Sales Tax for Remote Sellers](https://tax.wv.gov/Documents/TSD/tsd317.pdf)

## Taxable Goods
- **Are physical goods generally taxable?** Yes
- **Notable exemptions:**
  - **Food for home consumption (groceries):** **Fully exempt (0%)** since July 1, 2013. West Virginia phased out the grocery tax over several years: 3% in 2008, 2% in 2009, 1% in 2012, and 0% effective July 1, 2013. Exceptions: prepared food, candy, soft drinks, and alcoholic beverages remain taxable at the full 6% rate.
  - **Food purchased with SNAP/food stamps or WIC:** Fully exempt
  - **Prescription drugs:** Exempt
  - **Clothing:** Taxable at the full 6% rate (no general clothing exemption)
  - **Annual sales tax holiday:** West Virginia holds an annual sales tax holiday with exemptions for certain items. See [Sales Tax Holiday | WV Tax Division](https://tax.wv.gov/Business/Pages/SalesTaxHoliday.aspx)
- **Link to official exemptions:** [TSD 300 - Sales and Use Tax Exemptions (PDF)](https://tax.wv.gov/documents/tsd/tsd300.pdf)

## Local Taxes
- **Can municipalities add local sales tax?** Yes, municipalities can impose a local sales and use tax of up to 1%
- **Range of local rates:** 0% to 1%
- **Current municipalities with local tax:** Multiple municipalities impose a 1% tax. As of January 1, 2026, Clay, Franklin, Saint Marys, and Winfield also impose a municipal sales and use tax of 1%.
- **Remote seller local tax collection:** Yes, remote sellers must collect both state and applicable municipal sales tax
- **List of municipalities with local tax:** [Municipalities Imposing Sales and Use Taxes (PDF)](https://tax.wv.gov/Documents/SUT/MunicipalitiesImposingSalesAndUseTaxes.pdf)

Source: [Municipal Sales and Use Tax | WV Tax Division](https://tax.wv.gov/business/salesandusetax/municipalsalesandusetax/pages/municipalsalesandusetax.aspx)

## Filing Requirements
- **Filing frequency:** Assigned by the WV Tax Division based on registration information and sales volume:
  - Monthly
  - Quarterly
  - Annually
- **Filing deadlines:** Returns and payments are due on the 20th day of the month following the close of the reporting period. Annual returns are due on the 30th day following the close of the calendar or fiscal year.
- **Filing portal:** [MyTaxes - WV Tax Division](https://mytaxes.wvtax.gov)
- **Electronic filing:** [Electronic Filing for Businesses | WV Tax Division](https://tax.wv.gov/business/electronicfiling/Pages/BusinessElectronicFiling.aspx)
